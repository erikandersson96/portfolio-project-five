About the E-commerce store
We are a reseller of wrist watches. We resell watches from all types of brands like Seiko, Rolex, Breitling, Casio, Frederique Constant, and so on. Ranging from prices of $90 - $50.000.
About the company
Me Erik Andersson started this E-commerce store in the beginning of 2022.
I decided to start this E-commerce store after 2 tough years of covid-19 that affected our local store so bad that we sadly had to close.
But since a E-commerce store is much more cost effective than a local store located in the middle of our home town, I decided to focus on only online reselling.
This way I will not only save costs but I can also reach out to a much bigger client base.
I hope you will find the watch that you are looking for!
